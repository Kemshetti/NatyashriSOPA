About us

Natya Shri School of Dance specialises in Tanjavur style of Bharathanatyam, was found in 2020 in the city of Solapur, Bangalore by Smt. Veenashri Nimbargi.

MISSION


To take Bharatnatyam to each and every ethusiast wishing to learn this beautiful art form irrespective of caste , religion, nation, gender ,status or colour.Free education and scholarships would be provided to large number of students so they could afford learning dance and making a community of such beautiful dancers

VISION


To make dance forms especially bharatnatyam globally acclaimed and teach this graceful classical dance to every interested across the world. Help them develop love for the art and bring out that passionate dancer within them. Grow more and more love and respect towards indian rituals and traditions

CURRICULUM


Our education wing works towards offering a comprehensive dance curriculum for the learners and nurturing performers creativity. While the primary genre of instruction is Bharatanatyam, workshops in other dance forms, Music, Nattuvangam, Karnas and Yoga trainings are offered for well-rounded dance education.

ACHIEVEMENTS


Natya Shri is growing stronger with every passing day under the artistic direction of Smt. Veenashri Nimbargi, performing at the biggest platforms in our country and across the world in the mixed morden norm of virtual and live. The school regularly celebrates its Annual day and performs at its very own space, thereby providing a stage for their students to perform.

ClASSESS(Online and Physical)


Regular group classes are conducted both online and physical class based in solapure twice a week (one hour each) in batches. Also, for the benefit of students individual sessions are conducted. In addition to above, short term classes are conducted at the specific goal/purpose of the students/performers.
